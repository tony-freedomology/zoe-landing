21-Day Journey

First Steps

A New Believer Journey

The first weeks after saying yes to Jesus are some of the most important — and the most vulnerable. This journey gives you a daily companion for those first steps.

Start This Journey

21 Days

Duration

3 per day

Check-ins

21 foundations

Topics

New faith is tender. There are a thousand questions, a lot of excitement, and also a lot of uncertainty. What does it even mean to follow Jesus in an ordinary week?

This 21-day journey is built for exactly that season. Each day covers one foundational topic — from prayer to doubt to baptism to the long walk ahead. Zoe checks in three times a day with the passage, a reflection question, and space to actually think it through.

No app. No homework. Just a text message that meets you where you are.

The 21 Days

1
Psalm 139:1–16·You Are Known

“How does it feel to know that God has known you fully — before you knew anything about him?”

2
Matthew 4:18–22·What It Means to Follow

“Jesus said 'follow me.' What do you think that actually looks like in your everyday life?”

3
John 3:1–8·Born Again

“What's something in your life that you notice has already started to change since you said yes to Jesus?”

4
Ephesians 2:1–10·Grace Is Real

“In your own words, what is grace? How is it different from earning something?”

5
Matthew 6:5–13·Talking to God

“What's one honest thing you want to say to God today that you haven't said out loud yet?”

6
Psalm 119:105–112·Reading the Bible

“What's your honest relationship with Scripture been so far — exciting, confusing, both?”

7
John 14:15–17, 26·The Holy Spirit

“Have you experienced a moment where you felt led by something other than your own instinct? What was that?”

8
1 John 1:5–2:2·Sin and Forgiveness

“Is there something you're still carrying guilt over, even after becoming a Christian?”

9
1 Corinthians 10:12–13·Temptation Is Normal

“What's a recurring temptation in your life, and what would it look like to take the 'way out' God provides?”

10
Acts 2:42–47·The Church

“What's your relationship with church been like so far? What feels welcoming? What feels confusing?”

11
Hebrews 10:24–25·Community

“Who in your life do you feel like you could be honest about your faith with?”

12
Mark 9:24; John 20:24–29·Faith and Doubt

“What's a doubt or question about faith you've been carrying but haven't felt safe to ask?”

13
Psalm 100·Worship

“What's one specific thing about God that you genuinely feel grateful for today?”

14
Mark 10:42–45·Serving Others

“Is there someone in your life right now who needs something you could give — time, help, attention?”

15
2 Corinthians 5:17; Galatians 2:20·Identity in Christ

“What's one part of your old identity that you're still holding onto — that God might be asking you to let go?”

16
Romans 6:3–11·Baptism

“If you've been baptized, what does that mean to you now? If you haven't, what's keeping you from it?”

17
Luke 24:27, 44–47·The Bible as Story

“How does knowing that Jesus is the center of the whole Bible change how you read it?”

18
Psalm 27:13–14·Prayer That Waits

“What are you waiting for from God right now? How are you doing at waiting?”

19
2 Corinthians 9:6–8·Generosity

“What does generosity look like in your life right now — with money, time, or attention?”

20
1 Peter 3:15–16·Sharing Your Faith

“Is there someone in your life who doesn't know Jesus yet who you'd want to share this with?”

21
Philippians 1:6; Jude 24–25·The Long Walk

“What's one thing you want to carry forward from these 21 days? What does the next step look like for you?”
