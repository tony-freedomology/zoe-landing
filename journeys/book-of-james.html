14-Day Journey

Book of James

Faith in action. Wisdom from above. Fourteen days through one of the most practical books in the New Testament — one check-in at a time.

Start This Journey

14 Days

Duration

3 per day

Check-ins

14 readings

Bible Passages

James writes to a scattered church — people who claimed faith but lived differently from Monday to Saturday. His letter is less a theology lecture and more a direct conversation: you say you believe. Does your life show it?

This 14-day journey moves through the entire book, one passage per day. Each morning, Zoe sends you the passage. Each noon, a question. Each evening, a prompt to reflect on what you noticed. No app. No login. Just your phone and a text.

The 14 Days

1
James 1:1–4·Trials as Teachers

“What challenge in your life right now might actually be producing something?”

2
James 1:5–8·Asking for Wisdom

“Where do you most need wisdom today, and are you actually asking for it?”

3
James 1:9–11·The Rich and the Poor

“How does your relationship with money shape your trust in God?”

4
James 1:12–15·The Source of Temptation

“What desire, if left unchecked, tends to lead you astray?”

5
James 1:16–18·Every Good Gift

“What's something genuinely good in your life that you've forgotten to thank God for?”

6
James 1:19–21·Quick to Listen

“In your last difficult conversation, were you quick to listen or quick to speak?”

7
James 1:22–27·Doers of the Word

“Is there a truth you know but haven't acted on yet? What's stopping you?”

8
James 2:1–9·No Favoritism

“Who in your life do you treat as less important because of status, appearance, or wealth?”

9
James 2:10–13·The Law of Freedom

“How does the mercy you've received from God change how you treat others?”

10
James 2:14–26·Faith and Works

“What would someone know about your faith from watching your actions this week?”

11
James 3:1–12·Taming the Tongue

“What's something you said recently that you wish you could take back?”

12
James 3:13–18·Wisdom from Above

“Is the wisdom guiding your decisions right now from God — or from somewhere else?”

13
James 4:1–10·Humility Before God

“Where are you striving for something God hasn't given you? What would it look like to submit that?”

14
James 5:7–11, 19–20·Patient Endurance

“Who in your life needs someone to turn them back toward God right now?”
