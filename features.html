How It Works

Discipleship that fits inside real life.

Three check-ins a day. Two questions. No app. Zoe lives in your texts — and it remembers what you say.

Daily Rhythms

Dawn. Noon. Dusk.

Three times a day, at moments you choose, Zoe sends a short check-in. Not a broadcast. A conversation.

  • 🌅

    Dawn

    What are you bringing into today? Set an intention before the noise starts.

  • ☀️

    Noon

    How's it actually going? A mid-day anchor to the morning's intention.

  • 🌙

    Dusk

    What happened today? What are you carrying forward?

Good morning. What's one thing you want to bring before God today?

Two questions. Every time.

What is God saying to you?

Forces you to pay attention. Articulating it makes it real.

What are you going to do about it?

Closes the gap between knowing and doing.

Memory & Follow-Through

It remembers what you said.

When you tell Zoe on Monday that you're going to have that hard conversation — Zoe brings it back on Thursday.

When you tell Zoe something on Monday, it can bring it back on Thursday. That continuity across days is what actually changes people.

For Churches

Extends the pulpit throughout the week.

Church leaders can align Zoe with sermon content, so what lands on Sunday doesn't evaporate by Tuesday.

Sermon Alignment

Align Zoe's weekly prompts with your sermon series, so mid-week check-ins reinforce what your congregation heard on Sunday.

Aggregate Insights

Pastors see congregation-level trends — not personal messages. Know how your church is doing spiritually, not just who showed up.

No New Platform

It all happens in SMS. Your congregation doesn't need to download anything. Zoe meets them where they already are.

Trust & Privacy

Private by default.

Your conversations are yours. We never share them with your church leaders. Consent controls are yours to set.

Just between you and God

Your one-to-one conversations are not visible to church leaders by default. Ever.

Aggregate trends only

Church dashboards show trend-level health — not personal confessions or journal content.

Consent controls

You control memory depth, support access, and optional data-sharing settings.

Delete and export rights

Export your full spiritual history or delete everything. No hard feelings, no questions asked.
